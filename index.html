    About the App

    Charity Connect is an innovative platform transforming the way donations support orphanages in India. With over 10,000 orphanages facing financial struggles, the app provides a sustainable solution by utilizing 1% of unused storage space on smartphones to mine funds through eco-friendly Chia Blockchain technology. This unique approach requires no monetary contributions from users and aims to raise ₹88 Crores annually by engaging 72 million smartphone users. Transparency and trust are at the core of Charity Connect, with blockchain ensuring secure, tamper-proof transactions and real-time visibility of funds. Additionally, users are rewarded with revert points for their contributions, which can be redeemed for benefits like ad prioritization or partner rewards. By combining advanced technology with a noble cause, Charity Connect empowers users to make a meaningful impact effortlessly. It ensures funds are directly and fairly distributed to orphanages through automated smart contracts. Together, we can reshape the future of orphanage funding and secure a brighter tomorrow for thousands of children.

    Revolutionizing Orphanage Funding in India

    Our project aims to revolutionize funding for orphanages in India by leveraging unused storage space on smartphones to generate funds using the Chia Blockchain. With over 10,000 orphanages in India facing severe financial shortages, our solution ensures that essential care, education, and shelter for children are sustained. By utilizing just 1% of unused storage from smartphones, we enable users to contribute without any monetary donations, ensuring accessibility and inclusivity for everyone.

    Eco-Friendly Blockchain Technology

    The app utilizes Chia’s eco-friendly Proof of Space and Time mechanism, which is highly energy-efficient compared to traditional mining. This process allows funds to be generated without impacting device performance, ensuring users experience no computational strain. The blockchain-based system ensures every transaction is secure, tamper-proof, and fully traceable, fostering trust and confidence among users. Transparency is a core value, with live dashboards providing real-time updates on fund generation and allocation.

    Automated Fund Distribution with Smart Contracts

    Our solution incorporates smart contracts to automate fund distribution directly to verified orphanages based on pre-defined criteria, such as the number of children and financial requirements. Users also engage with the platform through an innovative reward system, earning revert points for their contributions. These points can be redeemed for partner rewards or prioritized ad placements, making participation both impactful and rewarding.

    Redefining Crowdfunding with Transparency and Incentives

    By combining eco-friendly blockchain technology with user engagement incentives, our project not only secures a steady stream of funding for orphanages but also ensures that contributions are transparent and accountable. This innovative model redefines traditional crowdfunding by addressing common challenges like trust issues, lack of transparency, and platform inefficiencies, creating a sustainable ecosystem that benefits orphanages and contributors alike.
